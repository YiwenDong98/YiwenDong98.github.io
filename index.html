About me

I am a student at Northeastern University working on my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I have experience doing full stack web development. I have tried a variety of different fields within and out of Computer Science.


One common theme in all my projects I come to realize is the idea of connectedness. Whether it be connecting information or people. I'm currently working on an eclipse project syncing plugin, sort of like google docs for eclipse. It'll make in person peer programing easier with the use of Operational Transformation. One of my earlier completed projects was an eclipse plugin to generate template and documentation as required for a fundamentals class at Northeastern. I've also created many websites connecting people to information with Mara, Carve, pollster.cc. I also created a traffic data visualization using Google Maps for the Boston area, connecting construction, accident, and congestion data to enhance the understanding of traffic problems plaguing the Greater Boston area. Another ongoing project is a Recumbent Bike that I am building for Sam who is disconnected to the world due to his disability. The hope is that an increase in his mobility will reconnected him to the outside world and his community. There’re many more projects I've worked on throughout the years on the more project page.
